Typing with ten fingers

May 17, 2020

Introduction

Using ten fingers to type text on a computer (touch typing) is fast and allows you to not look at your keyboard. I wholeheartedly recommend this practise to anyone using a computer on a daily basis. Being in IT, Iâ€™m often surprised of the low number of people aware of this typing technique. I learnt about it somewhat randomly, even though it is a tried and tested method!

Drawbacks

Advantages

The ten fingers are used uniformly across all keys, allowing for less wrist movement. Similarly, eyes donâ€™t need to alternate from screen to keyboard, allowing for less neck movement and more focus. As such, touch typing is ergonomic and results in an increase of productivity.

Technique

The idea here is to always press the same key with the same finger, all the while not looking at the keyboard. Both hands rest on the center row (home row). The F and J keys which correspond to the left and right index fingers respectively usually have a small mark on them.

Once you understand touch typing, make sure to force yourself to do it as soon as you use a keyboard. It will be hard work at first, but it will only get better ! Soon typing will become mechanical and just thinking a word will be enough for your fingers to type it.
